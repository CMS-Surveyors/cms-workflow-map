Every stage a customer travels through, from a fresh lead to a completed install, with each automation attached to the exact point it fires. Click a card to see its trigger, exactly how and where it pulls its information, and the precise message it sends. You can do three things from here: propose a new automation with the + button under any stage, suggest a change to an existing one, or ask Mack about the wording. None of these build anything on their own: they collect your notes, and when you press Copy for Mack it bundles them up to paste over to Mack, who builds them and folds them back into this map.
